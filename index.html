Talan's Trees

Our goal

We are providing a service this year to help you dispose of your tree after your Christmas and New Year festivities. All we ask back is a small donation towards our cause.

Between the 9th to the 11th January we will go around and collect everyone's trees to be recycled.

The money goes towards research into Rhabdomyosarcoma, a cancer that grows in the muscles and affects children of all ages.

As well as this we have been raising awareness about other cancers in children and run various activities bringing communities together.
